Chicken Fried Rice

Description

A simple, five-ingredient Chinese dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of chicken breasts
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1/3 cup frozen vegetables
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Sear the chicken and rice in a pan with olive oil
  4. Beat eggs together
  5. Combine the eggs with the rice and chicken. Fold in after a minute.
  6. Add soy sauce and frozen vegetables
  7. Serve
